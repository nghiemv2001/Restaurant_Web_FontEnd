Rustic Elegance Park

Rustic Elegance Park is a fine dining restaurant located in the serene countryside. The restaurant offers a picturesque view of the surrounding rolling hills and sits in a charming vintage farmhouse on the corner of Oakwood Lane and Country Road. Established in 2005, it underwent a complete renovation and redesign in the spring of 2020.

The restaurant has been owned by Chef Sarah Thompson since 2015 and has evolved significantly in both its cuisine and overall experience. Our tasting, five-course, and bar menus feature a delightful blend of locally-sourced ingredients. Guests can also visit the bar for light snacks, or simply enjoy a cocktail or a glass of wine.
